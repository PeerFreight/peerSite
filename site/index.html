All you need to know

What do you haul?

Hazmat freight is our main focus, and we also move reefer, dry van, flatbed, and specialized truckload across the lower 48 states.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. We hold active FMCSA broker authority under USDOT 5766712, we carry a $75,000 surety bond, and we are fully insured with contingent cargo, auto, general liability, and errors and omissions coverage. We are also a member of the TIA.

How do you vet carriers?

We verify every carrier's identity through Highway and check them against FMCSA SAFER for authority, insurance, and safety, and we do this on every load rather than only once when they sign up.

How fast do you pay carriers?

We pay our carriers within 24 to 48 hours, and we can do same day when a carrier needs it, so the best trucks tend to take our loads first.

How do I get updates?

We send you updates at pickup, in transit, and at delivery without you having to chase us, and you get a clean proof of delivery the same day the load arrives.

How do I send you a load?

You can send us a load by email or straight out of your TMS, whichever is easier for your team, or you can request a quote and we will take it from there.
