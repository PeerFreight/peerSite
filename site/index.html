All you need to know

What do you haul?

Hazmat is our focus, plus reefer, dry van, flatbed, and specialized truckload across the lower 48.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes — active FMCSA broker authority (MC# + USDOT 5766712), a $75K surety bond, and full coverage: contingent cargo, auto, general liability, and E&O. We're a TIA member.

How do you vet carriers?

Every carrier is identity-verified through Highway and checked against FMCSA SAFER before dispatch — authority, insurance, and safety — on every load.

How fast do you pay carriers?

Quick-pay in 24–48 hours, with same-day options — so the best trucks take our loads first.

How do I get updates?

Proactive updates at pickup, in transit, and delivery, plus a clean POD the same day. You never chase us.

How do I send you a load?

Email or your TMS — whatever's easiest. Or book a call and we'll get you set up.
