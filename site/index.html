Frequently asked questions

Here is what Peer can say before launch.

Is Peer currently arranging freight?

No. Peer’s operating authority is pending, and we are not accepting, arranging, or soliciting live loads. We will update this website when the required authority and launch controls are active.

Where will Peer operate?

Peer is preparing to operate as a U.S. freight brokerage, but launch lanes and freight scope have not been finalized. We will describe active coverage only after the relevant operating requirements are in place.

What kinds of freight is Peer preparing to support?

Peer is preparing for dry van, refrigerated, flatbed, tanker, and hazmat freight. Each shipment will still depend on launch scope, equipment, lane, commodity, insurance, and compliance requirements.

How will Peer handle problems in transit?

Peer is designing its service around clear ownership. When a pickup, appointment, equipment, or delivery issue occurs, the same team coordinating the load will work the exception and keep the shipper and carrier informed.
